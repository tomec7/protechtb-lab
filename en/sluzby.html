💾 NAS & Backup

What is NAS: NAS (Network Attached Storage) is a home or business server for centralized, secure file storage.

What you achieve: all data in one place with proper access rights for family/team members and regular backups.

Home examples:

  • Automatic backup of photos from the whole family’s phones.
  • Shared folders for documents, videos, and home projects.
  • Fast data recovery after deletion or laptop failure.

Business examples:

  • Unified company storage with role-based access rights.
  • Automatic backups of workstations and critical folders.
  • Recovery plan after incidents (ransomware, disk failure, user error).

We set up 3-2-1 backups, monitoring, and restore testing for long-term reliability.
